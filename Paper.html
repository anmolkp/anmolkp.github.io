"An Introduction to Hybrid Platform Mobile Application Development"


- International Journal of Computer Applications

PURPOSE OF WRITING

While selecting a platform for developing a Mobile Application, I wanted a platform which could provide the beauty of iOS and the reach of Android.

Researching on different platforms for publishing mobile applications introduced me to Hybrid Platform Mobile Application Development.

I had to read about the different frameworks and different languages for Hybrid Platform Application Development and select an appropriate framework for my application as well.

In order to make the journey of Hybrid Platform Mobile Development easier for future developers, I co-authored a paper on "An Introduction to Hybrid Platform Mobile Application Development" and published it in the International Journal of Computer Applications.
